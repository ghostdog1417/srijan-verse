Title: Moonlight Dreams
Author: Srijan Dwivedi
Date: 2026-03-19
Excerpt: Under the silver glow of the moon, memories dance...

Under the silver glow of the moon, memories dance,
Like fireflies caught in a glass of moments.
Each flicker a story, each glow a whisper of the past.

I trace your name across the starlit sky,
And the universe responds with gentle laughter.
In this solitude, I find a strange comfort—
A companion made of silence and silver threads.

The night holds secrets that the day refuses to keep,
And I am their guardian, their keeper of dreams.
When dawn breaks, I will return to the ordinary,
But until then, I belong to the moonlight.

These are my confessions to the stars,
My letters to the wind that will never be read.
Yet they matter, they persist, they glow—
Like the eternal dance of light against the darkness.
